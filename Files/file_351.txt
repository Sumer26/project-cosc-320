When the room went quiet, I could hear only the gentle panting of a dozen service dogs. Over the course of the next hour, the speakers got up one by one and went through the possible scenarios, while I fidgeted and frowned, taking notes when I could.During the question-and-answer segment at the end of the seminar, a woman stood up to speak into the microphone.
