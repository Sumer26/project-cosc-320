by
Perry Bard 
March 24, 2018January 4, 2021 
ShareShareEmailFacebookTwitterLinkedInMastodon 





Perry Bard, “Geography” (2018), graphite and rubber stamp on graph paper, 11 x 14 inches (courtesy of the artist)
In his brief tenure as Secretary of State, Rex Tillerson traveled to Brussels, Vienna, Paris, Tokyo, Seoul, Beijing, Da Nang, and Manila, but he never made it to Norway or Haiti…
