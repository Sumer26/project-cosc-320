It was the country’s largest display of defiance since the fall of Communism in 1989.The government’s decree decriminalizes corruption involving less than the equivalent of $48,000. Government plans could also free officials jailed for corruption, as well as prisoners with short jail sentences.Romanian officials said their plans are meant to reduce prison overcrowding and update the criminal code.
